Digestion Tips

  • check_circle

    The 20-Minute Rule

    Chew each bite thoroughly to initiate salivary enzymes. Aim for 20 minutes per meal.

  • check_circle

    Post-Meal Movement

    A 10-minute light stroll after eating helps regulate blood sugar.

  • check_circle

    Hydration Timing

    Drink water 30 mins before or after, but limit intake during meals.
